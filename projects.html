Welcome to Kisima Jangwani: Nurturing Hope, Empowering Lives

At Kisima Jangwani, our mission is not just to provide water; it's about cultivating hope and empowering lives in Kisima 78 Village, Isiolo, Kenya. Our projects are more than boreholes; they are pathways to sustainable solutions, education, and community empowerment.

Project Overview

Explore our initiatives in Kisima 78 Village, powered by IEEE and generously sponsored by IEEE HAC/SIGHT. Led by dedicated individuals—Kithinji Muriungi, Esther Muchiri, and @Peter Ebek—our projects aim to address critical challenges faced by the community.

Current Status

Witness the reality of Kisima 78 Village:

  • The old shallow well lies dormant.
  • Unsustainable individual wells dot the landscape.
  • A working borehole supports key community hubs.
  • Agricultural practices depend on unsustainable water sources.
  • Social challenges, poverty, and limited education opportunities persist.

Site Visit and On-Site Research

In February 2023, our IEEE SIGHT volunteers immersed themselves in Kisima Village, understanding the challenges firsthand. This user-centric approach guides our mission to provide sustainable solutions to the water crisis and beyond.

Identified Challenges

During the site visit, we uncovered pressing issues:

  • Water scarcity and unsustainable sources.
  • Social challenges, including teenage pregnancies and early marriages.
  • Tribal clashes over scarce resources.
  • General poverty prevalence.
  • Limited higher education access and career progression for youth.

Areas Open for Partnerships

Join hands with us in these crucial areas:

  1. Drill a Communal Borehole for domestic use, animal consumption, and crop farming.
  2. Equip the borehole with solar-powered technology.
  3. Construct a 10,000-liter water storage facility.
  4. Build water troughs for animals.
  5. Provide piping for community water access in a 500m radius.
  6. Offer mentorship on life, career, and technical skills to children and youth.
  7. Establish a public phone charging station and Wi-Fi spot for connectivity.

Considerations for Solutions

Our approach focuses on:

  • Facilitating sustainable economic activities.
  • Provision of reliable water sources.
  • Efficient energy sources for water pumping.
  • Transformative programs integrated into the project, emphasizing mentorship.
  • Adoption of sustainable social engagement activities.
